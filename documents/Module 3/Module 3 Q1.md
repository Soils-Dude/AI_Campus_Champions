Drafting grants is a very time consuming task that requires the culmination of thoughts, literature review, and concise writing abilities. This means that the final tweaking required to make a grant "ready to ship" will be in the hours instead of days it would normally take to craft a grant.

Background research (when looking for background info on a specific topic not necessarily something publishable) is another category that is high on my list. This is due to the intense nature of reading through and synthesizing peer reviewed papers or combing through many websites that don't necessarily give information up front. AI does a great job of pointing you in the right direction to determine the answer to a specific scientific question although it should be noted that all sources should be verified for accuracy.
